Dutch Oven Mountain Man Breakfast:

The Dutch Oven Mountain Man Breakfast is perfect for families and couples that are looking to get away to the mountains! It is filled with warm comfort food that will make even the hungriest man's man satisfied. You can count on this meal to fill the campground with an aroma that will attract not only the bears, but the other campers as well.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Mild Pork Sausage
  • 1 Mediun Onion, Chopped
  • 1 Clove Garlic, Minced
  • 1 Mediun Red Bell Pepper, Chopped
  • 1 Medium Green Bell Pepper, Chopped
  • 2 lbs. Frozen Shredded Hash Brown Potatoes
  • 12 Large Eggs, Beaten
  • 16 Oz. Shredded cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Build a campfire and allow the fire to burn until it has accumulated a bed of coals. Place a grate over the fire. Click here for a tutorial on how to build a fire from the experts at REI!
  2. Place a 12-inch cast iron Dutch oven onto the grate.
  3. Add sausage, onion, and garlic.
  4. Cook and stir until sausage is browned and crumbly and onion is translucent, 5 to 7 minutes.
  5. Stir in hash browns and bell peppers until well mixed
  6. Cook, stirring occasionally, until hash browns are hot and peppers are tender, about 15 minutes.
  7. Pour beaten eggs into the Dutch oven, allowing them to seep down to the bottom.
  8. Cover the Dutch oven and place it directly onto 5 to 10 hot coals.
  9. Place another 12 to 18 hot coals on top of the Dutch oven lid.
  10. Let cook until eggs are firm, about 40 minutes.
  11. Open the lid and sprinkle with Cheddar cheese.
  12. Cover and continue to cook until cheese has melted, about 5 minutes.
