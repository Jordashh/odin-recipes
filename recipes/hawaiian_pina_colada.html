Hawaiian Pina Colada:

Feature #1:Asour pineapple cut into a triangle garnished on the rim.

Feature #2:Creamy cocnout mixture blended to perfection.

Feature #3:A flavorful rum with fruit accents that dance on your tongue.

Finished off with an umbrella straw and beach view!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 C. Unsweetened Pineapple Juice
  • 1 Can (15 Ounces) Cream of Coconut
  • 1 1/2 C. Light Rum
  • 6 C. Crushed Ice
  • Pineapple Wedges

Steps

  1. In a 2-qt. pitcher, combine pineapple juice, cream of coconut and rum.
  2. Refrigerate, covered, until chilled.
  3. For each serving, add a generous cup of rum mixture and 1 cup ice to a blender.
  4. Process, covered, until smooth. Pour into a chilled hurricane or highball glass.
  5. Cut a 1-in. slit into tip of a pineapple wedge; slide wedge over rim of glass.
