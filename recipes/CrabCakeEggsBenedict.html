Crab Crake Eggs Benedict:

Layer #1:A toasted to perfection english muffin with 3 more layers of goodness atop.

Layer #2:A buttery & golden crab cake that is straight from the ocean.

Layer #3:A fluffy white egg poached to the perfect consistency.

Layer #4:A drizzle of creamy hollandaise that is bursting with lemon.

Topped with Green Chives!

Ingredients

  • 1 C. Greek Yogurt
  • 1 tsp. White Wine Vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tsp. Dijon Mustard
  • 1/2 tsp. Raw Honey
  • 1 tsp. Lemon Juice
  • 1/4 tsp. Salt
  • 4 Egg Yolks
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • Avocado
  • 1 Clove of Garlic, minced
  • 2 Cans of Crab
  • 1/4 C. finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 C. Mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp. chopped cilantro or parsley
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper
  • 1/2 C. Crushed Cracker or Breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp. Avocado Oil (recommended) or Olive Oil
